About The Dev

I have lived in the isoated city of Perth, WA, all my life. Born to two artists/teachers I have spent a large majority of my life absorbed in the world of art, dragged from gallery to gallery across Australia and Europe. To keep their child enterained on these overseas outings they bestowed upon me a DS when I was four. Ever since i've been playing and designing games. In highschool I discovered my love of board games, theatre, roleplaying games and visual arts, and was fortunate enough to find and befriend like-minded people to whom I owe a great deal for their support and creativity over the last decade.


My vision for my work practices is to create a small, independant game studio in which I am able to produce quality games both in the digital and tabletop spheres. Ideally these games address themes which I am passionate about, and take an artistic approch to game design highlighted by strong storytelling and thematic elements generated by mechanical systems which work to emphasise these ideas to audiences.

I acknowledge and pay respects to the traditional custodians of this land, Australia, upon which I am able to create art and tell stories.
